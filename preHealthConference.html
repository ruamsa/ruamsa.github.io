Pre-Health Conference

This year was RU AMSA's 13th Annual Pre-Health Conference!

Superheroes in Healthcare: Looking Beyond the Power of Knowledge

This year, our conference theme is Superheroes in Healthcare: Looking Beyond the Power of Knowledge. Our goal is to discuss what other qualities exist in standout healthcare workers, outside the realm of just medical knowledge. We hope that this increased awareness can start to implement progressive change and help cultivate a generation of compassionate healthcare workers.
