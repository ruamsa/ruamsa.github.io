UWorld Discount

We have partnered with UWorld to provide students with a discount. As you may know, UWorld is crucial in studying for the MCAT. It may be even expensive for some students. Due to this reason, UWorld is providing us a discount! We would love to help out more students so please pass this form on to any students that may be interested in getting a discount from UWorld for their MCAT prep. NJ Youth Leaders in Medicine

NJ Youth Leaders in Medicine provides virtual shadowing and volunteering opportunities, as well as information on different medical careers and live-streams with health professionals. They aim to spread useful information and help students in their endeavors to go into the field of medicine. Their goal is to help students that have had opportunities taken away from them due to the pandemic, underprivileged students, and students just wanting to learn more about the medical field. Check out their Instagram page, @ylm_nj !

Rutgers Blood Initiative

AMSA is proud to announce our collaboration with Rutgers Blood Initiative (Instagram: @rutgersbloodinitiative ), a budding organization and a chapter of the national org, University Blood Initiative, at Rutgers with various healthcare and volunteering opportunities. As an RWJ partner, their job is very important right now since the pandemic has caused a serious depletion of blood in healthcare. It is an urgent matter, and your potential involvement in their organization would be a great opportunity to get involved on campus and help others!

MCAT 101 Volunteering

MCAT 101 is a new platform designed to engage students in the process of their prep and encourage them to learn from each other and have fun! Students often spend hundreds and thousands of dollars during this time when these resources could be provided for free and learned from one another. Here at MCAT 101, we are trying to create an organization to provide free tutoring services and resources for students.

You do not have to be interested in the MCAT at all to even help though! If you are interested in any volunteer/internship opportunities for the fall, we have a place for you. Some opportunities may be in our premed blog, running our account as a student support team, answering any science questions students may have, editing our website, or even developing our team further through business. You can see more information on our website here.

We are a great fast-paced team and are looking for passionate people that want to make an impact and help others. Come join us! If you are interested in volunteering, fill this out! If you would like updates when our tutoring program and resources launch, sign up for the waitlist here!
